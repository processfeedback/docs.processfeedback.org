# How to Identify AI-assisted Writing Patterns 

---

When exploring a student's Writing Process Report, teachers can use the pointers below to identify potential AI-assisted writing. 

**1. Inspect active typing time in the *Typing Time* section**  




**2. Inspect large "external" paste events in the *Paste Events* section**  

A student may paste text generated by AI into their editor during their writing process. Here is how to identify such paste events:
1. In the Paste Events section of the report, click on the "Characters" column to sort, so that large paste events appear on top.
2. Look for rows that have "Likely from Outside" in the "Source" column.
3. Click on "Pasted Text" in the row and inspect if the text appears to be from outside source.
4. Click on the "Time of Paste" to open Compare Changes and inspect where in the editor this text text was pasted.

**3. Inspect text deletions in the *Text Added and Removed* section**  

Check how often deletions took place and what was deleted in the Text Added Removed section.

**4. Inspect breaks taken in the *Active Typing Sessions* section**  



**5. Inspect free typing and revision patterns in the *Edited Sentences* section**
