# How to Identify AI-assisted Writing Patterns 

---

When exploring a student's Writing Process Report, teachers can use the pointers below to identify potential AI-assisted writing.

**1. Find the largest "external" paste event in the Paste Events section**  

A student may paste text generated by AI into their editor during their writing process. Here is how to identify such paste events:
1. In the Paste Events section of the report, click on the "Characters" column so that large paste events appear on top.
2. Look for rows that have "Likely from Outside" in the "Source" column.




2. Check how often deletions took place and what was deleted in the Text Added Removed section.
